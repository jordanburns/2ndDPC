Diversified Plastics Corporation

Construction Components

Our company has a distinct advantage of not only molding the foam block, but also having the capabilites of molding the interior web in our injection molding facility (Accurate Plastics).

Diversified Plastics Corporation is a proud molder of Insulating Concrete Forms (ICFs) including the Amvic Building System. ICFs offer a builder faster, easier installation and a homeowner the peace of mind of having superior insulation, and wind, fire and bug resistance. Interlocking blocks coupled with secure rebar positioning system allows unsurpassed structural strength.

Key Benefits

  • Greatly enhanced energy efficiency (R30+)

  • Type 2 Flame retardant (fire rating 3 hours)

  • Greatly increased structural strength
