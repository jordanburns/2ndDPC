Materials

Expandable Polypropylene (E.P.P.)

E.P.P. is a resilient, durable foam with marvelous cushioning properties and phenomenal ‘memory’ that returns it to its original shape after being deformed by a blow or static weight. Because of these properties, it excels as a packaging material and is durable enough to provide an exceptional material for reusable dunnage. These properties also make it the optimum for use in high-impact interior and exterior components for automobiles.

  • Wide range of densities (1.3 to 10 P.C.F.)
  • Ideal for fragile or heavy items needing packaging
  • Durable and reusable

Expandable Polystyrene (E.P.S.)

E.P.S. is an inexpensive cushioning material that is commonly used for all sorts of shape-molded and fabricated packaging. It also features such a high degree of thermal protection properties that it is commonly used for insulated shippers and coolers. Because it can be molded in a variety of densities, it is also used to mold products of all descriptions.

  • Excellent shock absorption for packaging
  • Density ranges from 1.125 PCF to 3 P.C.F.
  • Lightweight for decreased shipping cost
  • Recyclable

Expanded Polyethylene (E.P.E.)

Expanded Polyethylene is a closed cell foam, which is flexible and lightweight. An environmentally-friendly product, it can be recycled easily, and is 100% non-toxic. Expanded Polyethylene Foam provides outstanding energy absorption characteristics, weight to strength ratio, high thermal resistance, and are resistant to water, oils and most chemicals and are available in a variety of densities.

  • Superior compressive strength
  • Moisture and weather resistance
  • Recyclable

Expanded Copolymer (P.S./P.E.)

All materials molded in a variety of densities to meet your cushion or structure needs. Expanded copolymer has the insulative properties of E.P.S., with the durability of E.P.P.

  • Good for packaging delicate materials
  • Lightweight
  • Durable
