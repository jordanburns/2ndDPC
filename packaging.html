Packaging

Benefits of DPC Packaging

  • Custom made parts to fit your needs
  • Low cost per piece
  • Variety of impact resistance to keep your parts safe
  • Shape molded and fabricate cut parts avaliable

We can design and mold a variety of packaging to fit your product, manufacturing, and shipping needs. Using a wide variety of materials we engineer the best protection for your product at the lowest possible cost.

We can mold end caps, trays, corner protectors, self-contained shippers or complicated packaging components for a variety of products no matter the weight, fragility, size or packing line requirements involved.
