That's DPC! We are dedicated to making sure our products meet the best standards for you, the customer. We strive to create and deliver your product at the lowest rate possible, without sacrificing quality. Just tell us what you need, and we can make it happen.

Diversified Plastics Corporation is a custom molder of all types of plastics, from expandable foam co-polymers to injection grade plastics. DPC was founded in 1969 and has been in continuous operation ever since.

Today its facilities encompass three of the largest state-of-the-art molding factories in the United States.

Since its beginning, DPC has been a leader of plastics industry in the fields of innovations, quality molding, setting standards for service and timeliness, as well as developing cutting edge technology.

DPC can apply secondary operations to products such as, but not limited to: hot melt, push pins, butyl tape, and cotton shoddy.

DPC owns a tool shop, started in the year 2000. All tools cut in the shop are CNC, cut from plate aluminum. The tool shop is capable of maintaining and revising obsolete items.

DPC owns 31 trailers and operates 13 trucks. This enables DPC to pick up raw materials and deliver customer product at a more competitive rate. Our #1 goal is on time delivery.

Diversified Plastics Corporations is certified to meet all automotive needs in the industry. We are dedicated to the highest quality product, with the lowest environmental cost.

DPC has been in Nixa, Missouri since the very beginning, when it was founded in an abandoned cheese factory. In 1973, DPC built a new facility in Nixa after moving into injection molding. In 1976, there was a second molding facility constructed in Centralia, Missouri. To this day, we still call Nixa home.
